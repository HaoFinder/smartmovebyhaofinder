How to Unlock Your KaribuBoma Tier 3 Verification

July 24, 2026 8 min read By KaribuBoma Team

In the world of trust-based hospitality, verification is everything. KaribuBoma's Tier 3 verification — the SmartMove Trusted badge — is the highest level of trust you can achieve on the platform. It signals to the community that you are not only who you say you are, but that your identity has been physically verified. This guide walks you through every step of unlocking your Tier 3 status, so you can host with confidence and travel with peace of mind.

What is KaribuBoma Tier 3 Verification?

Tier 3 verification is the pinnacle of the KaribuBoma trust framework. It combines all the benefits of Tier 1 (phone verification) and Tier 2 (M-Pesa name/ID match) with an additional layer: physical address verification performed by SmartMove agents or movers. This is the equivalent of a "verified address" badge, giving hosts and surfers the ultimate confidence in your profile.

Tier 3 unlocks:

  • ✨ Featured host badge — your profile gets priority visibility
  • 🔒 Highest trust level — maximum safety for you and your guests
  • 📈 Priority match ranking — you appear higher in search results
  • 🤝 Exclusive community access — connect with other verified members

Why Tier 3 Matters for Hosts and Surfers

In a community where money never changes hands for stays, trust is the currency. Tier 3 verification is the ultimate signal that you are a reliable, trustworthy member of the KaribuBoma ecosystem. For hosts, it attracts higher-quality guests who are serious about cultural exchange. For surfers, it opens doors to the most welcoming and trusted homes across Kenya.

With over 1,200 active hosts and 2,340 successful stays, KaribuBoma has already proven that trust-based hospitality can thrive. Tier 3 is your ticket to being part of the most trusted circle within this growing community.

What to Expect During Address Verification

The SmartMove Trusted address verification is designed to be smooth and non-intrusive. Here's what happens:

  • Booking: You schedule a time that works for you via WhatsApp or the SmartMove platform.
  • Visit: A verified SmartMove agent visits your address at the scheduled time.
  • Validation: The agent confirms your physical presence at the address and matches it with your profile details.
  • Completion: Your Tier 3 badge is activated immediately, and you receive a confirmation notification.

The entire process takes less than 15 minutes and is conducted with complete discretion and professionalism. Your privacy is our priority.

Cost and Payment

Tier 3 verification comes with a small annual fee to cover the cost of physical address verification. The fee is KES 350 (~$2.70) per year, payable via M-Pesa. This is a one-time annual charge that ensures the highest level of trust for the community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Tier 3 verification take?

Once you schedule your address verification, the entire process typically takes 1-2 business days. The visit itself is under 15 minutes, and your badge is activated immediately after validation.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. All verification data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties. The address verification is conducted by SmartMove agents who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Can I skip Tier 1 and 2 and go straight to Tier 3?

No, Tier 3 builds on Tier 1 and Tier 2. You must complete all previous verification steps to unlock Tier 3. This layered approach ensures the highest level of trust and safety.

What if I don't have an M-Pesa account?

M-Pesa is the most widely used mobile money platform in Kenya. If you don't have an account, we recommend setting one up — it's quick and easy. Tier 2 verification requires an M-Pesa name match to confirm your identity.
