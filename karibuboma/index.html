The Future of Hospitality in Africa

KaribuBoma is reimagining how travelers experience Kenya. By connecting visitors with verified local hosts for free stays, we're building a community rooted in trust, cultural exchange, and shared humanity. Unlike traditional accommodation platforms, KaribuBoma operates on a zero-fee model—no nightly charges, no booking fees, and no financial transactions between hosts and guests for the stay itself.

The platform is built on a 3-Tier Verification System that progressively unlocks features: from basic phone verification to full SmartMove Trusted status with physical address verification. This multi-layered approach addresses the primary barrier to home-sharing—safety and identity assurance—by integrating directly with Kenyan identity vectors like M-Pesa and National ID.

KaribuBoma is part of the SmartMove ecosystem, cross-selling services like luggage transport, cleaning, and local tours. This creates a sustainable revenue model without compromising the core value proposition: free, authentic stays with locals.

With over 1,200 active hosts across 47 counties and a 4.9-star average rating from 2,340 successful stays, KaribuBoma is already proving that trust-based hospitality can thrive at scale. Whether you're a digital nomad, a budget traveler, or a curious adventurer, KaribuBoma invites you to experience Kenya through the eyes of those who call it home.
