🏦 What is a deposit loan (rent financing)?

A deposit loan (also called rent deposit financing) is a short-term loan that covers your rental deposit – typically 2 months' rent. Instead of paying KSh 200,000+ upfront, you pay just 1 month's rent to move in, and repay the deposit portion in affordable installments over 3-12 months. SmartMove partners with licensed lenders to offer this exclusively to our customers.

⚙️ How SmartMove deposit loan works

  1. Find your home – Browse properties on SmartMove or tell us your requirements via WhatsApp.
  2. Apply for deposit loan – Fill a 2-minute online form (no paperwork, no collateral).
  3. Instant approval – Receive decision within 4 hours (most within 1 hour).
  4. Move in – Pay only one month's rent to the landlord. SmartMove pays the deposit directly to the landlord.
  5. Repay in installments – Monthly deductions via M-Pesa or standing order over 3, 6, or 12 months.

✅ Eligibility & requirements

SmartMove deposit loan is designed to be inclusive. Basic criteria:

  • Kenyan citizen or resident with valid ID
  • Age 22-65 years
  • Regular source of income (employed or business with bank statements)
  • Minimum monthly income: KSh 30,000
  • Clear CRB record (no active default)
  • The property must be verified through SmartMove (we handle landlord confirmation)

No collateral required. No co-signer needed for amounts under KSh 150,000.

📉 Interest rates: 3% APR explained

Our partner lenders offer 3% APR (Annual Percentage Rate) for 12-month loans – one of the lowest in Kenya for unsecured consumer loans. For comparison:

  • SmartMove deposit loan: 3% APR (effective ~0.25% per month)
  • Traditional bank personal loan: 13-18% APR
  • Mobile loan apps (Fuliza, etc.): 60-120% APR equivalent
  • Shylocks: 10-30% per month

Example: Borrow KSh 150,000 for 12 months at 3% APR = total interest ~KSh 2,400 (less than a dinner for two).

❓ Frequently asked questions

Does SmartMove give the loan directly?

SmartMove facilitates the loan through our licensed lending partners. We negotiate the 3% APR exclusively for our customers.

What happens if I move out before repaying the loan?

You remain responsible for the remaining balance. The deposit is refunded by your landlord to the lender at end of tenancy (minus any deductions).

Will this affect my CRB rating?

Timely repayments improve your credit score. Late payments are reported to CRB like any other loan.

Can I use the deposit loan for any property in Nairobi?

Yes – all properties listed on SmartMove or verified through our agents qualify. We currently serve Nairobi, Kiambu, and Machakos counties.

SmartMove guarantee

If your deposit loan application is rejected, we'll help you find a property within your budget that requires lower deposit (some landlords accept 1+1).
