🛋️ Strategy #6: Furniture rental vs buying — Save KSh 50,000 upfront

Rent, don't buy (for short stays)

From KSh 8,500/mo

Buying furniture for a 1-bedroom apartment costs KSh 75,000-150,000. Renting the same from SmartMove costs KSh 8,500-15,000/month.

If you're staying less than 15 months, renting is cheaper. Even if you stay longer, you can apply rental payments toward purchase after 6 months.

Your savings: KSh 50,000 - 100,000 upfront cash preserved

📅 Strategy #7: Timing your move — Off-season discounts

Move during off-peak months

10-20% cheaper

Moving demand in Nairobi peaks in December-January (holidays) and May-August (school breaks). Move during February-April or September-November for:

  • 10-20% lower mover rates
  • More availability (better crews)
  • Negotiating power with landlords (lower rent)

Your savings: KSh 5,000 - 15,000
